Our classes

Beginner

Basic techniques and padwork. No sparring and the club provides gloves.

Advanced

More advanced techniques and padwork. With sparring. Gloves, shinpads, and mouthguard required (not provided).

Fighter

Longer sessions for fight-ready members

Cardio

Cardio and strength training adapted to Thaiboxing

Children's Class

Thaiboxing is a unique and fun way for children to enhance not only their physical but also their mental strength. Training includes exercises that improve the childrens' strength, balance and conditioning.
